Welcome Message From Your Rep

From the Desk of the Director General

Welcome to the official page of Honourable Chimaobi Sam Atu, dedicated to political support and engagement in Enugu North and South Federal Constituency. As the esteemed House of Representatives Member representing our constituency, Honourable Chimaobi Sam Atu has been a beacon of progress, dedication, and unwavering commitment to the betterment of our community. This platform serves as a hub for information, updates, and opportunities to collaborate in shaping a brighter future for our constituency. Join us in our journey to advance our region and empower our people through active political involvement and support. Together, we can achieve great strides and bring about positive change.

Dear fellow citizens of Enugu North and South Federal Constituency, We stand at a pivotal juncture in the trajectory of our community's development, and the moment has never been more significant for collective action and support. It is with great enthusiasm that we extend an earnest appeal to each one of you to join our support organization for Honourable Atu, the dedicated House of Representatives Member representing our constituency.
